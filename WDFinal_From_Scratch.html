The Cool Factor

No one can stop admiring airplanes. Almost every airplane in the sky has it's own unique paint job. This gives the airplane character and style.

The Proof

No airplane ever wanted to hurt anyone. Although it is sad to see some airplanes take a tumble, it isn't usually their fault. Airplanes are usually nice to the pilots and passengers and always want to do the job right.
